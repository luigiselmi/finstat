Notes on Investment Management Specialization
=============================================
The specialization on [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/investment-management), from the University of Geneva,  is composed of 5 courses:

1. Understanding financial markets
2. Meeting investors’ goals
3. Portfolio and risk management
4. Securing investment returns in the long run
5. Planning your client’s wealth over a 5 years horizon

## Corso 1: Understanding Financial Markets
This course consists of four two-hours modules. You must pass four quizzes, one for each module. It should be possible to complete it in two days, such as a weekend.

1. General introduction and key concepts
2. Major financial markets
3. Other financial markets
4. Financial markets and the economy
