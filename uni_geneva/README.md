Notes on Investment Management Specialization
=============================================
The specialization on [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/investment-management), from the University of Geneva and UBS,  is composed of 5 courses:

1. Understanding financial markets
2. Meeting investors’ goals
3. Portfolio and risk management
4. Securing investment returns in the long run
5. Planning your client’s wealth over a 5 years horizon

## Course 1: Understanding Financial Markets
[Course 1](https://www.coursera.org/learn/understanding-financial-markets/) consists of four two-hours modules. You must pass four quizzes, one for each module. It should be possible to complete it in two days, such as a weekend.

1. [General introduction and key concepts](c1m1_understanding_financial_markets.ipynb)
2. [Major financial markets](c1m2_major_financial_markets.ipynb)
3. [Other financial markets](c1m3_other_financial_markets.ipynb)
4. [Financial markets and the economy](c1m4_financial_markets_and_economics.ipynb)
